Getting Started

Click on the "Get Started" button to create an account. Fill in the required information and click "Register" to create your account. Once you have created an account, you can log in using the "Sign In" button.

Competing with Friends

Based on your role, join a team or create your own team to compete with friends and colleagues. Earn digital badges and rewards as you complete your milestones and hit your targets. Climb to the top of the leaderboards and level up the fun!

Creating Habits

Once you have logged in, you can create new habits by clicking on the "Create Habit" button. Enter the habit name, description, and frequency. You can set daily, weekly, or monthly goals for each habit.

Tracking Progress

Track your progress by logging your daily activities. Click on the "Log Activity" button to record your progress for each habit. You can view your progress over time with helpful charts and graphs.
