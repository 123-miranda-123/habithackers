Help

Getting Started

Click on the "Get Started" button to create an account. Fill in the required information, choose your role, and click "Register" to create your account. Once you have created an account, you can log in using the "Sign In" button.

Team Members

If you are a team member, you will first be prompted to join your own team by entering in the team ID number. Afterwards, you will be redirected to your member dashboard.

➢ Creating Individual Habits

All team members can create new habit goals by clicking on the "Create New Team Habit" button. Choose the habit name, enter the frequency (goal) and the time interval. You can set daily, weekly, or monthly goals for each habit.

➢ Enter Progress

Enter in your progress for each of your habit goals. You will also be able to see the progress for your team and company.

➢ User Settings

Click user settings in the navigation bar to reset your password and/or delete your account.

Team Captains

If you are a team captain, you will first be prompted to create your own team. Afterwards, you will be redirected to your team captain dashboard. There you will find your team name and team ID number for members to enter in when they sign up!

➢ Modify Team Settings

Click the "Rename Team" button to rename your team. You also have the option to remove members from your team.

➢ Creating Team Habits

All team captains can create new habit goals by clicking on the "Create New Team Habit" button. Choose the habit name, enter the frequency (goal) and the time interval. You can set daily, weekly, or monthly goals for each habit.

➢ User Settings

Click user settings in the navigation bar to reset your password and/or delete your account.

Administrators

All company administrators will immediately be directed to the admin dashboard.

➢ User Settings

You will be able to add, edit, and delete all users in the company.

➢ Team Settings

You will be able to add, assign captains to, and delete all teams in the company.

➢ Creating Company Habits

You can create new habit goals for the entire company by clicking on the "Create New Company Habit" button. Choose the habit name, enter the frequency (goal) and the time interval. You can set daily, weekly, or monthly goals for each habit.

Logout

Click the "Logout" button in the top right corner to log out of your account.
